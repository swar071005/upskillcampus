================================================================================
🎵 HOW TO USE THE PLAYER
================================================================================

1. PLAY A SONG:
   - Double-click any song in the playlist
   OR
   - Select a song → Click the large ▶ button

2. PAUSE/RESUME:
   - Click the ⏸ button (changes to ▶ when paused)

3. NEXT/PREVIOUS:
   - Click ⏭ for next song
   - Click ⏮ for previous song

4. SHUFFLE:
   - Click 🔀 to toggle shuffle mode
   - Button turns PINK when active

5. REPEAT:
   - Click 🔁 to toggle repeat mode
   - Button turns PINK when active

6. VOLUME:
   - Drag the volume slider (🔊)
   - Range: 0-100%

7. PROGRESS:
   - Progress bar shows song progress
   - Automatically updates while playing

  ├── Main Class (JFrame)
  │   ├── createTopPanel()     → Album art + song info
  │   ├── createControlsPanel() → Buttons + progress
  │   ├── createPlaylistPanel() → Glass panel + list

  │
  ├── Inner Classes:
  │   ├── GradientPanel        → Gradient background
  │   ├── CircularAlbumArt     → Vinyl record design
  │   └── GlassPanel           → Frosted glass effect
  │
  └── Event Handlers:
      ├── togglePlayPause()

      ├── toggleShuffle()
